Principles
How We Teach
01

Show the derivation

No magic. Every formula is derived from first principles with every step visible. If you can follow the algebra, you own the concept.

02

Numbers before notation

Work through a concrete example first, then generalize. Five data points and a calculator beat abstract theory every time.

03

Free, forever

No paywalls, no premium tiers, no login walls. Knowledge that's locked away isn't knowledge — it's a product. This is not a product.
